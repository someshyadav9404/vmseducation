Our Mission:

The mission of the school is to provide an environment that lays emphasis on intellectual and moving growth in which every student discovers and realizes his/her full potential. Where emphasis is laid on integrity, ethics, moral courage, hard work responsibility and self-discipline.

Our mission is:

  • To innovate in educational technology.
  • To adopt novel and relevant techniques.
  • To improve the classroom learning and skill development.
  • To make the students better human being and responsible citizens of the society.
  • To encourage the students to have independent spirit to explore new roles, ideas and strategies.

Our Vision:

Education is the most powerful tool leading to self awareness and empowerment. Our vision is to preserve and enhance the highest standards of excellence, preparing the generation next to give shape to their dreams. We try hard to provide enriching knowledge to prepare enlightened citizens of the world sensitized to environmental concerns, feeling of patriotism, communal unity and society at large. VMS aims to be a foundation of excellence where sound ethical values, character wisdom, leadership, service and academic achievements are encouraged in a safe and nurturing environment.
